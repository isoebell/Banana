Work

Weather Room is an interactive installation project; a colour visualisation of sound. This installation intends to bring emphasis to the importance of human connect, and highlight the warmth experienced when we engage in our relationships and step away from our digital vortexes.

Click the image on the right and interact with our installation; you could use a projector of your own, or just watch it on your computer and listen to this soundscape, and experience the changes in relation to sound.
